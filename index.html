Consciousness Retreat 2026

A week long retreat on the most fundamental question

This retreat brings together researchers from neuroscience, cognitive science, philosophy, machine intelligence and the arts to explore the nature of consciousness in a collaborative, informal setting. Through presentations, dialogues, and interactive sessions, we seek to foster novel perspectives, training and enduring collaborations.
