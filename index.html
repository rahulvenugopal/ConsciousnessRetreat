Retreat 2025

Consciousness & Cognition Research Retreat

This retreat brings together researchers from neuroscience, cognitive science, philosophy, and the arts to explore the nature of consciousness in a collaborative, informal setting. Through presentations, dialogues, and interactive sessions, we seek to foster novel perspectives and enduring collaborations.

Program

July 25 10:00–11:30
Opening Circle

Welcome to the retreat. This session sets the tone, introduces participants, and invites reflection on our shared inquiry into consciousness.

July 25 12:00–13:30
Embodied Consciousness

A discussion on how bodily states and sensorimotor experiences shape our conscious perception and subjective reality.
